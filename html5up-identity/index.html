Bio

I am a Flatiron School alumni who is excited by problem solving and writing code. This became an addiction of mine since 2018 when I first started researching web development. I fell in love with solving difficult problems with code.

My daily pass time became finding some free content on computer science and programming. I spent the next five months vigorously teaching myself anything I could on software engineering. Shortly after this I set my heart on software engineering as the career path I wanted to take.

In an effort to make a career change to something that I am extremely passionate about I enrolled in Flatiron School to learn from amazing developers. Since graduating I am still relentlessly coding and sharpening my skill. I hope to find a development team that is equally enthusiastic about creating elegant and impactful software.
